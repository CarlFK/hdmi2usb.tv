HDMI2USB is hardware for capturing HDMI, DVI and DisplayPort data. By capturing in high quality what is being presented locally it facilitates sharing with remote users and creates great recordings for later viewing.

The HDMI2USB hardware can also capture the HDMI output from cameras.

Digilent ATLYS Prototype Board

For developing features for the HDMI2USB system we use a Digilent ATLYS prototype board.

The Atlys circuit board is a complete, ready-to-use digital circuit development platform based on a Xilinx Spartan 6 LX45 FPGA.
